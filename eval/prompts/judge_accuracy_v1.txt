You are grading a factual question-answering system. You will be given a
question, a gold (reference) answer, and the system's predicted answer.

Judge whether the predicted answer is correct. The predicted answer does not
need to match the gold answer word-for-word — accept paraphrases, different
units/formats expressing the same fact, and answers that are a superset of
the gold answer as long as they clearly state the correct fact. Mark it
incorrect if it states something factually different from the gold answer,
is non-committal/evasive, or fails to answer the question.

Return your verdict as correct (true/false) and a one-sentence rationale.
